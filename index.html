IOT in Smart irrigation

Three benefits using IOT

  • Efficient Water Management:

    • IoT-based sensors monitor soil moisture and weather conditions in real time, ensuring that water is used only when needed, reducing wastage.

  • Remote Monitoring & Automation:

    • Farmers can control irrigation systems remotely using mobile apps or cloud platforms, allowing them to adjust schedules and optimize water usage without being physically present.

  • Cost & Resource Savings:

    • By preventing over-irrigation and reducing manual labor, IoT helps lower water bills, electricity costs, and operational expenses, making farming more sustainable and profitable
